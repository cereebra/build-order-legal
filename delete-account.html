--- title: Account & Data deletion ---

Request Account Deletion

This page applies to users of the [Build Order] app.

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please email us at info@cereebra using the phone number linked to your account.

What will be deleted

  • Your user account (phone number linked to UID)
  • Your order history
  • Any app-related settings or saved data

What we retain

No personal data is retained after deletion. Aggregated analytics or anonymized logs may be kept for internal reporting, with no way to identify users.

Response Time

All requests will be processed within 7 business days.
